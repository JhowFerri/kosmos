# kosmos
A tree of knowledge — explore life, minerals and languages through interactive cladograms

## Motivation
I love biology, specially zoology, and when I understood that life was organized in clades and that all of that created a gigantic tree of life, I wanted a place that I could see that tree and navigate it seeing all clades and all species that we knew, but unfortunately I haven't found anything like that on the internet. So, I decided to build myself that great site I imagined in my head. Then I thought: there are more things around there that can be classified in the same way, languages are a good example, so it could be a big tree of knowledge. The greek name Kosmos means Universe and organization. It's also a homage to the brilliant writer and scientist Carl Sagan.

> "The cosmos is within us. We are made of star-stuff." — Carl Sagan

## Features Planned
* Species registration
* Dynamic cladograms
* Species and clades descriptions
* Extensible to any hierarchical knowledge domain — languages, minerals, and beyond
